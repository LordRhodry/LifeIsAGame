The goal is to find 2 number card that would allow to complete the equation.

Each time you balance an equation you score 1 point, a new equation to complete is chosen, and you go again.

The winner is the player with the most point when all cards are gone.

Each player plays one turn and then the next player plays.
If the cards do not fulfil an equation they are returned face down and the next player's turn begins.
